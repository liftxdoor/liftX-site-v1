The Repair Process

Identify what failed—and what caused it.

Door problems are often several conditions adding up. LIFTX checks balance, spring sizing, cables, drums, tracks, rollers, sections, hardware, operator setup, and safety devices before deciding what the repair should include.

Safety comes first

LIFTX will not perform a repair that leaves the door structurally unsound or unsafe.

No fear-based selling

Normal wear is not presented as an emergency. Immediate concerns are explained clearly.

Repair Services

Broken Springs
