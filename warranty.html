Warranty Structure

Clear written coverage—not vague promises.

Manufacturer warranties apply separately to eligible products. LIFTX’s warranty addresses qualifying workmanship performed directly by LIFTX.

1-Year Craftsmanship Coverage

Covers qualifying installation and workmanship performed directly by LIFTX.

Three Maintenance Visits

For qualifying covered work, visits are scheduled approximately every four months during the one-year period.

Manufacturer Coverage

Eligible doors, parts, springs, glass, electronics, and operators may carry separate manufacturer warranties.

Important: Coverage may be affected by unauthorized service or adjustment, work performed by another company or maintenance provider, misuse, impact damage, structural movement, weather-related damage, improper maintenance, or conditions outside LIFTX workmanship. The written warranty supplied with the work controls.
